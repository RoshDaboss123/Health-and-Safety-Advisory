Health and Safety Advisory

What Is Our Mission?

Our mission is to keep people informed about any dangers that might affect the safety of others. People can use this informative website to see which places are safe to visit and which places to limit travel. You can use this website to make sure that you are safe in restaurants, stores, and other public places you frequently visit.

Why did we make this Website?

This website was built because of the recent events of COVID-19. We want to expand this idea into other types of problems in our society. Our everyday lives have been drastically changed due to COVID-19, so we want to make it as safe as possible for everybody else.
